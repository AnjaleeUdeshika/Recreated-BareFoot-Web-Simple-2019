Welcome to Barefoot Gallery

The Barefoot Gallery Colombo serves as a platform for artista,musicians,poets and filmmakers.The space is used in variety of ways:exhibitions by local and foreign artists and photographers,concerts,poetry readings,film nights and theater.The cafe on its premises hosts private dinners and parties at night and is home to Colombo's most popular pub quiz.

After hiatus,it reopened in 1991 as "Gallery 706 Colombo" and was the first commercial art gallery in Sri Lanka.The salon type atmosphere prevalied and Gallery 706 hosted a number of exhibitiona in the 1990's by artists such as Jagath aweerasinghe,Chandraguptha Thenuwara,Anoli Perera, and Muhanned Cader. It also presented exhibitions of work by visitors,either those living temporarily in the island or others just passing through.Three notable exhibitions were a retropective by George Classen in 1993,an exhibition of acrylics on paper by Ivan Peries in 1996,bothwere members of the illustrious 43 Group that was founded by Lionel Wendt in 1943;And,a comprehensive exhibition of Lionel Wendt photographs that went up for view and sale-a request from the owners of the collection that the directors of Gallery 706 were happy to oblige.150 pictures were selected from a substantial 600
